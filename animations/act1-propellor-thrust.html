🔬 What's Happening?

When the fan spins: It pushes air molecules downward (blue dots moving down)

Newton's 3rd Law: Air pushes back upward with equal force

Result: The paper experiences LIFT and rises up!

Drone Connection: Propellers work exactly the same way - they push air down to create lift that keeps the drone flying! 🚁
